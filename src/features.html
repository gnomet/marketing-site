    • Marketplace types
    Product sales, rentals, services - we have it all.

    Need a marketplace to sell products? Is your idea to facilitate peer-to-peer rentals? Or maybe a site for booking services? No worries - we support all types of marketplaces! And your merchants and buyers can be individuals or companies.

    Payments
    Global payments made simple. Setup takes only a few minutes.

    Sharetribe's built-in payment system allows your merchants to accept both PayPal and all of major credit cards. It works in 190+ countries and in 25+ currencies. You can easily earn commission from each transaction.

    Manage & Track
    Manage your marketplace. You are in control.

    Easily manage and moderate your marketplace from our admin dashboard. Connect Google Analytics to get a detailed view of what is going on.

    listings
    Selling and renting made easy.

    It couldn't be easier for people to create new listings on your marketplace and accept payments. They can add all the product information, including pricing, geolocation and multiple photos, and manage their listings effortlessly.

    browsing
    Find the information you need.

    Sharetribe provides advanced tools for categorizing and filtering content. People can browse listings as a list or on a map. Content can be searched by location or using keywords. You can set up categories, subcategories, and numerous custom filters to help people find what they are looking for.

    profiles & storefronts
    A storefront for everyone.

    Every member of your marketplace will have their own profile page, which they can turn into a full-fledged storefront. Storefronts will display their bio, listings, and reviews from buyers. Members can follow each other to get information when new content is added.

    White label design
    Your concept, your brand. Add your logo, custom domain & branding.

    Your marketplace should look like you. Our tools make it easy to add your custom branding: logos, cover photos, color themes, and a custom domain. You can even add custom CSS to make the marketplace match the look & feel of your brand.

    Mobile friendly
    Your marketplace on the go. Optimized for every screen.

    Sharetribe marketplaces are designed to look good on all screen sizes. Your members get a great experience, whether they're on desktop, tablet, or a smartphone. The content will load quickly on any device.

    Language & currency
    Feel like home, wherever you are.

    Sharetribe works all over the world. Our payment system supports 190+ countries and 25+ currencies. Our platform is available in multiple languages, including English, French, German, Spanish and Portuguese. Didn't find your language? Use our easy translation tool to translate it yourself!

    Access control
    Safe & secure.
    As it should be.

    You can control who has the ability to view, buy, and sell in your marketplace. Make it open for everyone, restrict it to people with an invitation, or only allow in people with certain email addresses. Allow your team to manage the marketplace for you by making them administrators. With Sharetribe, it's easy to control who has access to what.

    communication
    Messaging & order management. Simple & effective.

    Your members can connect with each other through messages. Every member will have their own inbox inside your marketplace. The inbox also helps them keep track of the orders they receive.

    Trust & reputation
    Build a trusted community.

    You can allow people to write reviews after each transaction. This helps in weeding out the bad apples and building trust in your marketplace community.

    Marketing, Social media & SEO
    Spread the word. Be social.

    Use Facebook Connect to get more signups. Allow people to easily share on Facebook & Twitter. Link your blog to your marketplace and build visibility by crafting quality content. Reach out to your community by sending marketing emails. All Sharetribe marketplaces are SEO optimized so your content will get good visibility in search engines.
